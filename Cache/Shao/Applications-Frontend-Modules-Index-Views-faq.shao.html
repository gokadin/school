Account questions
Why can't I log in?
Make sure that you wrote the right username
and password, and check for Caps Lock.
What happens to my account when I cancel my subscription?
Your account information will remain for 30 days,
should you decide to come back with us.
Financial questions
Do I need to enter my credit card information when I sign up?
You have a 30 day trial period. After that, if you decide to stay with us, you will have to enter your credit card information, unless you choose the free version.
What methods of payment do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards and paypal.
Other questions
Can my students access the web site as well?
Yes. We offer many features that require your students to login.
For example we allow for students to pay for their tuition directly on the website.
What is SSL encryption?
It is a security measure that validates the identity of the website.
This way, a potential attacker might have a hard time posing itself as a certain wesbsite to steal your information.
